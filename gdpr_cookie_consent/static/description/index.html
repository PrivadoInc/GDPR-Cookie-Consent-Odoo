How to Setup Privado Cookie Consent in Odoo website?

Privado Inc

Step 1: Sign Up in Privado Cookie Consent
  • Sign up here https://app.privado.ai/signup
    • Step 2: Scanning the website:
  • Here you scan the website and generate a list of cookies that your website uses. We auto-categorize these cookies into categories and you have to categorize the unknown cookies.
    • Step 3: Designing a cookie banner:
  • Design your banner wherein you inform users of the use of cookies and provide CTA of Accept, Deny, and Cookie Settings.
    • Step 4: Setting up a consent policy:
  • Setup a geo-policy for consent ensuring compliance with each country or region's privacy laws.
    • Step 5: Generate privado's js script:
  • Publish and generate a javascript that you need to paste in "Privado Cookie Script" under feature. Make sure Privado's GDPR Odoo Module is active before performing this step
